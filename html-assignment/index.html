About Me

My hometown is Xi'an, Shaanxi, China, a city with a history of over two thousand years. You may not have heard of its name, but you have probably heard of the Terracotta Army of Emperor Qin Shi Huang located there. I earned a Bachelor's degree in Electronic Engineering from NWPU in China in the early 21st century and have worked for 25 years since then. My career experience is quite diverse. I have been an electronic engineer, manufacturing engineer, sales director, general manager of a software company, and a professional outdoor adventure guide. I have also had two entrepreneurial experiences.

At the age of 45, I decided to leave China, where I spent the first half of my life, and move to Canada to explore a completely new field of study, which is IMD. My goal is to live the life I want to experience. My life journey has led me to uphold one belief:

True peace and joy in the heart are the greatest forms of happiness.-Zhongnan Jia
